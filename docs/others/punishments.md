# Punishments

All penalties are issued at administrator discretion based on severity, intent, and impact. This page serves as the authoritative reference for punishment guidelines. Repeat offences typically result in escalation to the next tier.

## Penalty Tiers

**Tier 1 - Warning**

**Tier 2 - Minor Penalty**

**Tier 3 - Moderate Suspension**

**Tier 4 - Major Suspension**

**Tier 5 - Permanent Ban**

## Infractions

### Code of Conduct

- **Minor sportsmanship violation** 
- **Harassment** 
- **Discriminatory language or symbols**
- **Content involving minors** 
- **Witch hunting** 
- **Withholding information for competitive advantage** 

### Game Integrity

- **Cheating (aimbot, ESP)** 
- **VAC / Game ban (TF2)** 
- **Banned third-party software** 
- **Banned scripts**
- **Game exploits (minor)** 
- **Game exploits (cheating-level)**
- **Map or weapon exploits**
- **Match fixing — team captain/leader**
- **Match fixing — all other involved players**
- **Ghosting** 
- **Improper server administration** 

### Accounts & Identity

- **Alternate accounts / account sharing**
- **Ban evasion / false identity**
- **Playing on a permanently banned player's account** 

### Match Conduct

- **No-show without prior communication** 
- **Failure to provide demos** 
- **Undeclared ringer** 
- **Pause abuse** 
- **Repeated forfeits** 

## Additional Notes

- **Top division players (Invite)** receive stricter penalties as outlined in the General Ruleset Section 2.5. Where a specific uplift is noted (e.g. discriminatory language), it is listed above.
- **Team penalties**: When a team is found collectively responsible (e.g. knowingly rostering an ineligible player), the team may face match forfeits, point deductions, or disqualification in addition to individual penalties.
- **Cheating in non-official contexts** (PUGs, scrims, public servers): penalties still apply but may be reduced at admin discretion.
- Offence history does not reset between seasons.

## Appeals

Players may appeal any penalty through the official league support channels. Appeals must be submitted within **7 days** of the penalty being issued.

A pending appeal does not suspend the penalty. The player remains banned while the appeal is under review.
