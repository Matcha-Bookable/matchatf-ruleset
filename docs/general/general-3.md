# Penalties & Registration

## Section 4: Penalties, Warnings, and Suspensions

Penalties are issued at administrator discretion based on the severity, intent, and impact of a rule violation. Repeat offences typically result in escalating penalties.

**General tiers:**
- **Warning**: issued for minor or first-time unintentional violations
- **Minor team penalty**: applied to a team for violations that affect competitive play
- **Suspension**: temporary league ban for the individual, length depending on offence
- **Permanent ban**: reserved for the most severe violations (cheating, CSAM, repeat offences)

Specific ban lengths are listed under the relevant rule sections above.

## Section 5: Player Registration and Eligibility

- Players must register with an active Steam account in good standing (no relevant VAC bans).
- Players may only be registered to one team at a time per competition.
- Players found to be registering under a false identity or evading a ban will be subject to a 24-month ban minimum, doubling on repeat offences.
- Eligibility requirements specific to each format (6v6 and Highlander) are outlined in their respective format rulesets.
- If a player loses control of their main account and must use an alt account, they must notify their divisional admin and complete IP verification checks. Discord streaming to prove identity is also an accepted alternative.

## Section 6: Team Registration and Eligibility

- Teams must maintain a minimum roster size as defined by their format ruleset (6 for 6s, 9 for Highlander).
- Teams are responsible for ensuring all rostered players meet eligibility requirements.
- Teams found to have knowingly rostered an ineligible or banned player may face forfeits, penalties, or disqualification.

### Team Withdrawal

If a team withdraws from a competition mid-season:

- The team is **immediately disqualified**. All of their remaining matches are voided.
- Results of matches already played against the withdrawing team are also **voided** and removed from standings.
- The **team leader/captain** receives a **2-season ban** from captaining any team in the league.

Voluntary withdrawal without notifying admins in advance will be treated more severely at admin discretion.

## Section 7: Transfer Window and Roster Lock

Player additions and roster changes are subject to the following rules throughout the season.

### Open Window

At the start of each season, rosters are open. Teams may add and remove players freely until the roster lock comes into effect.

### Soft Roster Lock

A soft roster lock takes effect once finalized division tiers are published. After this point:

- All new player additions require **admin approval**.
- Players may only be added as late joiners if their experience level is appropriate for the division. A player's experience is determined by the highest division they have played at least **3 matches** in across the current or last 3 active seasons.
- Players already on the roster may continue to play without restriction.

### Full Roster Lock

A full roster lock comes into effect at the start of the **final week of the regular season**. No new players may be added to any roster until the season ends.

### Playoff Roster

Only players rostered before the full roster lock are eligible for playoff matches.

### Lock Lifted

The roster lock is lifted once the season's official wrap-up post is published, at which point teams may freely modify their rosters for the next season.
